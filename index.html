About Us

The Cognitive Lab, headed by Assistant Professor Rajlakshmi Guha, Rekhi centre of excellence for the science of happiness, IIT Kharagpur. Our interdisciplinary research covers a wide range of issues in brain and cognition, eye tracking, cognitive assessment through speech and language, and neuroimaging. We integrate behavioral experiments with similar research on patients with cognitive disorders.
