Our Research

At the Cognitive Science Laboratory, we focus on a range of research areas including:

  • Brain and cognition
  • Eye tracking
  • Cognitive assessment through speech and language
  • Neuroimaging
  • Behavioral experiments with patients with cognitive disorders

Our research integrates various methodologies to understand the intricate workings of the human mind and its implications on cognitive disorders.
